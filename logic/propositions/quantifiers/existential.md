# The Existential Quantifier

## Definition

If p is a proprosition over U with T<sub>p</sub> ≠ ∅, said "There exists an n in U such that p(n).", denoted (∃n)<sub>U</sub>(p(n)) or, in short, (∃n)(p(n)). The symbol ∃ is the existential quantifier.
