# The Negation of Quantified Propositions

## The negation of an existential quantifier

¬(∃x)(p(x)) ⇔ (∀x)(¬p(x))

## The negation of an universal quantifier

¬(∀x)(p(x)) ⇔ (∃x)(¬p(x))
